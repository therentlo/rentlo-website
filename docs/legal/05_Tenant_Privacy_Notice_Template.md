## Why Your Data Is Collected

Your landlord uses your data to:

- Prepare and manage your tenancy agreement

- Send payment reminders
- Track payments and manage any arrears
- Communicate about maintenance and property issues
- Keep records required by law (tax records, LHDN reporting)
- Resolve disputes if they arise

## The Legal Basis for Using Your Data

Under Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA), your landlord processes your data based on:

- **Your consent** — by signing the tenancy agreement and this notice
- **The tenancy contract** — managing the tenancy requires processing your data
- **Legal requirements** — Malaysian law requires certain records for tax and dispute resolution
- **Legitimate interest** — your landlord has a legitimate interest in managing their property and protecting against non-payment

## Your Rights

Under PDPA, you have the right to:

| Right | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Access** | Ask your landlord for a copy of the data they hold about you |
| **Correction** | Ask your landlord to fix anything that's wrong |
| **Withdraw consent** | Tell your landlord to stop processing (this may affect the tenancy) |
| **Object** | Say no to specific processing (e.g., automated reminders) |
| **Limit processing** | Ask your landlord to restrict how they use your data |
| **Complain** | File a complaint with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Department |

**To exercise your rights:** Contact your landlord at **[YOUR EMAIL]**.

## How Your Data Is Protected

Your data is protected through:

- Encrypted storage and transmission in Rentlo's platform
- Limited access — only your landlord (and their authorised team) can see your data
- Secure authentication and access controls
- Proper disposal of any printed documents containing your data

If a security breach affects your data, your landlord will:
- Notify you within a reasonable time
- Explain what happened and what steps to take
- Work with authorities to limit the impact

Through Rentlo, you'll receive automated messages from your landlord including:
